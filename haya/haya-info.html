SUZUKI HAYABUSA

ENGINE

The Suzuki Hayabusa is powered by a 1,340cc, inline four-cylinder, liquid-cooled DOHC engine. It delivers strong torque, smooth power delivery, and the ability to reach extremely high speeds, making it one of the most powerful production motorcycles.

WHEELS & TIRES

The Hayabusa comes with lightweight alloy wheels fitted with high-performance radial tires. These tires provide excellent grip, stability, and handling in both straight-line speed and cornering.

BODY

The body of the Hayabusa has a sleek and muscular design. It is built using strong yet lightweight materials, giving it durability and agility while maintaining its aggressive, sporty look.
