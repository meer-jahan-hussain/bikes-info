BMW S 1000 RR

ENGINE

The BMW S1000 RR is powered by a 999cc inline four-cylinder engine that redefines superbike performance. Featuring BMW’s ShiftCam technology for variable valve timing and lift, the engine delivers exceptional power across the entire rev range—combining explosive acceleration with refined efficiency. With a peak output of over 200 hp, it’s built for track domination and unmatched road performance.

WHEELS & TIRES

recision handling starts with lightweight cast aluminum wheels on the S1000 RR, enhancing agility and stability. These are paired with high-grip Pirelli Diablo Supercorsa SP or similar sport tires, offering excellent traction and confidence under aggressive riding. For riders seeking even more performance, optional carbon wheels further reduce rotating mass for quicker turn-in and improved responsiveness.

BODY

The S1000 RR features an aggressive and aerodynamic design that mirrors its racing pedigree. Its asymmetrical styling has evolved into a more refined, purposeful look, with sharp lines and a focused rider triangle. The bodywork not only looks race-ready but also contributes to ergonomics, weight savings, and thermal efficiency, keeping the bike cool and comfortable during intense rides.

AERODYNAMICS

Aerodynamic efficiency is a key focus in the S1000 RR’s design. Integrated winglets on the front fairing provide added downforce for increased stability at high speeds and during hard braking. The sleek, wind-tunnel-tested profile minimizes drag, enhancing both performance and fuel efficiency. This advanced aero design ensures the bike remains planted and predictable, even at full throttle.
